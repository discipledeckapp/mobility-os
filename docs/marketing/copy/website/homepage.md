## Features Section

### Driver Verification

**Headline:** Know exactly who is driving your vehicles — before they get your keys.

**Body:**
Mobiris verifies every driver with a biometric liveness selfie and government ID check — NIN, BVN, Ghana Card, or Kenyan national ID. Guarantor records are captured and verified separately. Driver documents are stored, reviewed, and version-tracked.

The fastest path: send the driver a secure self-service link. They complete everything from their Android phone. You see the results on your dashboard in real time.

---

### Remittance Tracking

**Headline:** Every payment, confirmed or disputed, with a timestamp.

**Body:**
Record each driver's daily remittance. Confirm when cash is received. Raise a dispute if there's a shortfall — add a note, it's on the record. Waive when appropriate. Your operational wallet shows the running balance for each business entity at any point in time.

No more "I paid, you didn't record it" disputes with nothing to show for it.

---

### Fleet & Assignments

**Headline:** Assign drivers to vehicles in seconds. See the status in real time.

**Body:**
Create an assignment, start it, complete it, cancel it — from the web or the mobile app. Every assignment has a start time, end time, and a complete log. Your operational readiness report tells you what percentage of your fleet is active right now and flags drivers whose licences expire in the next 30 days.

---

### Cross-Operator Fraud Detection

**Headline:** A driver who defrauded another operator won't slip through at yours.

**Body:**
Mobiris maintains a shared identity graph across all operators on the platform. When a driver with a fraud history tries to onboard at your company, we surface the risk — without exposing any other operator's records.

This is the one thing you genuinely cannot build on your own. It only works because multiple operators are on the same platform.

---

### Mobile Field App

**Headline:** Your drivers, connected. In the field, offline if needed.

**Body:**
The Mobiris mobile app (iOS and Android) lets drivers view their assignments, submit remittance, and complete self-service verification — from their phone. Built for low-end Android devices with offline-first design for areas with intermittent connectivity.

## How It Works

**Headline:** You're up and running in under a day.

1. **Set up your fleet structure** — add your business entities, fleets, and vehicles. Decode VINs automatically.
2. **Onboard your drivers** — send each driver a self-service link. They complete documents and biometrics. You review and approve.
3. **Start dispatching** — create assignments, start them, track completion.
4. **Collect and reconcile** — record daily remittance, confirm or dispute, watch your wallet balance in real time.
5. **Stay compliant** — licence expiry alerts, full audit trail, operational readiness report on demand.
