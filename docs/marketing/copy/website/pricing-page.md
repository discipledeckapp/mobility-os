## FAQ

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. New accounts get 14 days free on the Growth plan — no credit card at signup. After 14 days, choose a plan or your account moves to read-only mode. Your data is kept for 90 days.

**How do I pay?**
Via Flutterwave or Paystack — card or bank transfer. We also accept wallet top-ups; your subscription is settled from your platform wallet balance automatically. Enterprise customers can request invoice-based billing.

**How does per-vehicle billing work?**
Your base subscription includes the vehicles in your plan. Each vehicle above that is billed at ₦1,500/month (Growth). A vehicle counts as "active" if it has been in an assignment at any point in the billing period.

**What's a driver verification?**
A verification is one biometric liveness check + one government ID cross-check for a single driver. Growth plans include 10/month; additional ones are billed at ₦600 each. Verifications are separate from driver *records* — you can create a driver record without verifying them (relevant for Starter).

**Can I change plans?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

**Is my data shared with other operators?**
No. Your operational data (drivers, vehicles, remittance, assignments) is completely isolated to your account. The cross-operator intelligence layer returns only risk signals — a score, not records from another operator's account.

**Is biometric data stored securely?**
We store biometric data as encrypted embeddings only — no raw selfie images are retained after verification. Consent is captured from each driver at onboarding. Our architecture complies with Nigeria's NDPA, Kenya's Data Protection Act, Ghana's DPA, and South Africa's POPIA.

**Do you offer a Data Processing Agreement for NDPA compliance?**
Yes, on request. Available on Growth and Enterprise plans. Contact us at support@mobiris.ng.

**Do you have a referral program?**
Yes. Refer another fleet operator and you both get 1 month free on your next bill. No limit on referrals.
