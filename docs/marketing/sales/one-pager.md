## The Problem

Fleet operators in Africa — running minibus fleets in Lagos, matatu SACCOs in Nairobi, trotro operators in Accra — are managing millions in daily remittance from WhatsApp groups and paper notebooks.

The result: **10–20% remittance leakage per month**, unverified drivers, no audit trail, and zero compliance visibility. For a 50-vehicle operator collecting ₦15M/month in remittance, that's ₦1.5M–₦3M disappearing with no record of where it went.

There is no purpose-built software for this segment. Telematics tools track vehicles, not remittance. Generic ERP doesn't fit the vehicle-for-hire model. Spreadsheets don't scale. The operators keep losing money they can't see.

---

## What Mobiris Does

Mobiris is the **risk infrastructure for vehicle-for-hire transport companies** — the verification, accountability, and fraud intelligence layer that operators run on.

| Module | What operators get |
|---|---|
| **Driver Verification** | Biometric liveness verification + NIN/BVN/Ghana Card/KE ID cross-check at onboarding. Guarantor capture and independent verification. Driver self-service OTP link — drivers verify themselves from their Android. |
| **Fleet & Vehicles** | Vehicle catalog with automatic VIN decode. Status lifecycle management. Operational readiness report. Licence expiry alerts. |
| **Assignments** | Driver-to-vehicle assignment with full lifecycle tracking — start, complete, cancel — all timestamped. |
| **Remittance** | Record, confirm, dispute, waive — with timestamps on every action. Running operational wallet ledger per business entity. |
| **Cross-Operator Intelligence** | Shared identity graph across all tenants. A driver who defrauded one company is flagged at the next — via a risk score, not raw records. |
| **Mobile Field App** | Driver-facing iOS/Android app for assignments, remittance, and self-service verification. Offline-first for low-connectivity areas. |
