# 30-Day Execution Plan

## Week 1: Foundation and First Signal

### Day 1

- create Google Sheet tabs and columns
- define ICP dropdown values
- load first 40 raw leads from manual research

### Day 2

- enrich first 20 leads with Codex
- identify A and B leads
- write first transport-operator landing page draft

### Day 3

- send first 10 personalized messages
- publish 1 blog article
- log all messages in `Activities`

### Day 4

- enrich next 20 leads
- publish 1 LinkedIn post and 1 X thread
- create 3 outreach variants for transport operators

### Day 5

- send next 10 personalized messages
- review first reply patterns
- update pain signals and scoring notes

## Week 2: Build Rhythm

- source 10 to 20 raw leads per day
- enrich at least 15 per day
- send 20 messages per day
- publish 2 articles this week
- repurpose each article into LinkedIn, X, and short-form script
- launch one checklist lead capture page on WordPress

## Week 3: Double Down on What Gets Replies

- drop lowest-performing message angle
- focus 70 percent of outreach on top 2 ICPs
- create one hire-purchase campaign page
- create one logistics fleet campaign page
- publish one comparison-style article and one operational checklist article

## Week 4: Tighten Conversion

- review booked demos and no-shows
- update CTA and landing-page friction points
- create objection-handling snippets
- align outreach with strongest article topics
- prioritize leads closest to activation

## Daily Workflow

### Morning

- source raw leads
- enrich with Codex
- prioritize top 20

### Midday

- send personalized outreach
- publish or schedule one content asset

### Afternoon

- log activity
- send due follow-ups
- review replies

### End of day

- capture insights
- update sheet
- prepare tomorrow’s queue

## Target Metrics for First 30 Days

- 250 to 400 leads sourced
- 150 to 250 leads enriched
- 300 to 400 outreach messages sent
- 8 to 15 percent reply rate
- 3 to 6 percent positive reply rate
- 5 to 15 demos booked
- first few signups with activation tracked

## Success Criteria

- clear winning ICP emerges
- clear winning pain angle emerges
- at least one content theme starts generating relevant interest
- outreach and content are feeding each other instead of operating separately
