# Content Pillars

## 1. Core Content Strategy

Mobiris content should do three jobs:

- make operators feel understood
- show practical control and visibility outcomes
- move readers toward a demo, checklist, or contact form

## 2. Pillar Map

### Pillar A: Driver trust and verification

Topics:

- how to know who is actually driving your vehicle
- why weak driver records create loss
- when guarantor records help and when they fail
- how to structure verification without slowing operations

Best formats:

- SEO articles
- LinkedIn posts
- short checklists
- customer-style scenarios

### Pillar B: Assignment control and accountability

Topics:

- vehicle handover discipline
- reducing “nobody knows who had the car”
- assignment records for growing fleets
- how to reduce disputes around use, damage, and responsibility

Best formats:

- blog articles
- landing page copy
- short videos
- operational checklists

### Pillar C: Remittance, repayment, and recovery visibility

Topics:

- how to track who paid and who did not
- why manual remittance tracking breaks at scale
- hire-purchase visibility for operators and financiers
- using structured records to reduce loss

Best formats:

- SEO articles
- LinkedIn carousel/text posts
- WhatsApp-style snippets
- downloadable scorecards

### Pillar D: Fleet operations sanity

Topics:

- what operational chaos looks like in a real fleet
- simple controls that reduce surprises
- the cost of weak visibility
- moving from notebooks and WhatsApp to structured operations

Best formats:

- founder posts
- story-style blog articles
- short form scripts
- landing page copy

## 4. Repurposing Workflow

### Article -> LinkedIn post

- pull one painful insight
- use one strong example
- end with one question or simple CTA

### Article -> X thread

- use 6 to 10 short posts
- keep each point plain and specific
- no abstract strategy language

### Article -> short-form script

- 20 to 45 seconds
- start with one hard truth
- give one practical fix
- close with a soft CTA

### Article -> landing page section

- convert insight into:
  - problem headline
  - short supporting paragraph
  - 3 bullet proof points
  - CTA
