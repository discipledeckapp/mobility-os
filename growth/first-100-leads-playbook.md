# First 100 Leads Playbook

## Goal

Build the first 100 useful Mobiris leads without scraping, spam, or bloated tooling.

The target is not volume first. The target is:

- clear ICP fit
- visible operational pain
- reachable decision maker
- enough context to personalize

## Lead Mix Target

- 35 transport operators
- 25 delivery/logistics fleets
- 25 asset finance / hire-purchase operators
- 15 corporate fleets

## What To Collect Per Lead

- full name
- title
- company
- source URL
- one public signal
- one pain hypothesis
- one outreach angle

## Source Lanes

### Lane 1: LinkedIn manual discovery

Use manual search for:

- `fleet manager Nigeria`
- `transport operations manager Lagos`
- `head of fleet logistics`
- `vehicle finance operations`
- `collections manager vehicle finance`
- `transport company owner`
- `delivery operations manager`

What to look for:

- operating role
- growth or hiring posts
- compliance or control language
- evidence of real fleet activity

### Lane 2: Company websites

Look for:

- transport company pages
- logistics fleet pages
- lease-to-own or finance pages
- service fleet pages

What to capture:

- what they actually do
- where they operate
- whether they mention drivers, compliance, finance, recovery, tracking, or fleet scale

### Lane 3: Public directories and associations

Examples:

- logistics directories
- local business directories
- fleet or transport association pages
- verified business listings

### Lane 4: Search-driven discovery

Search examples:

- `fleet management company Lagos`
- `staff bus operator Nigeria`
- `delivery fleet company Lagos`
- `vehicle hire purchase Nigeria`
- `logistics fleet company Nigeria`

### Lane 5: X/Twitter manual discovery

Look for:

- business updates
- complaints about driver control
- growth announcements
- transport or logistics operations commentary

## Qualification Checklist

A lead is good enough to keep if:

- the company clearly operates vehicles or manages drivers
- the contact appears tied to operations, finance, compliance, or ownership
- there is at least one believable operational pain hypothesis
- there is at least one credible outreach angle

## Daily Target

### 60-minute sourcing block

- 10 raw leads collected
- 5 fully enriched
- 5 queued for later enrichment

### 60-minute outreach block

- 5 to 10 high-quality messages sent

## Founder Script For Quick Manual Notes

When you find a lead, capture notes like this:

```text
Company: [NAME]
Contact: [NAME]
Role: [TITLE]
URL: [URL]
What they do: [1 line]
Why they might care: [1 line]
Pain signal: [1 line]
Outreach angle: [1 line]
```

## What To Avoid

- leads with no fleet relevance
- junior contacts with no path to influence
- generic “CEO” lists with zero context
- copying giant volumes of weak-fit names into the sheet

## Success Standard

The first 100 leads should produce:

- clearer ICP ranking
- repeatable pain language
- first replies
- first demos
- first high-signal objections
