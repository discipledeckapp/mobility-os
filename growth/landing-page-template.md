# Landing Page Template

## Page Types

- ICP landing page
- campaign landing page
- checklist/report download page
- demo booking page

## Core Structure

### 1. Hero

Headline:

- name the pain directly

Examples:

- `Know who has the vehicle. Know what is owed.`
- `Reduce fleet losses caused by weak driver and remittance visibility.`
- `Bring structure to driver verification, vehicle assignment, and repayment tracking.`

Subhead:

- explain the outcome in plain language

Primary CTA:

- `Book a demo`
- `Get the checklist`

Secondary CTA:

- `See how it works`

### 2. Problem section

Use 3 to 5 bullets:

- you are not sure who had the vehicle
- payments are hard to reconcile
- driver or guarantor records are not trustworthy
- operations rely on scattered chats and spreadsheets

### 3. What Mobiris helps you control

- driver identity and verification status
- structured vehicle assignments
- remittance and hire-purchase visibility
- accountability history

### 4. Use-case section

Build variants for:

- transport operators
- logistics fleets
- hire-purchase operators
- corporate fleets

### 5. Outcome section

Examples:

- fewer assignment disputes
- better follow-up on obligations
- faster visibility into who paid and who did not
- less dependence on memory and manual reconciliation

### 6. Social proof section

If early-stage and proof is thin, use:

- operational scenarios
- product screenshots
- “built for” statements
- founder credibility

### 7. CTA close

Examples:

- `If your operation is growing and visibility is getting weaker, let’s walk through your current workflow.`
