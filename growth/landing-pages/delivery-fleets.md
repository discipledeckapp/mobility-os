## Problem

Delivery fleets move fast.

That speed creates a hidden risk when:

- driver records are weak
- handovers are informal
- accountability lives in chats
- teams are not fully sure who had what and when

The result is confusion, slow follow-up, and avoidable disputes.

## What Mobiris Helps You Control

- verified driver records
- structured vehicle assignments
- visible assignment state
- accountability history tied to the right person

## Why This Matters

As delivery activity increases, weak handover discipline becomes expensive.

Not because the team is careless.

Because the workflow is too dependent on memory and informal updates.
