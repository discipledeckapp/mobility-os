## Problem

You are likely dealing with some version of this already:

- you are not fully sure who paid and who did not
- driver records are incomplete or hard to trust
- vehicle handovers are easy to discuss and harder to prove
- guarantor details are on file, but not strong enough when needed

At smaller scale, this feels manageable.

As the fleet grows, it starts costing real money and time.

## What Mobiris Helps You Control

- driver verification status

- remittance accountability
- unresolved shortfalls and disputes

## Why This Matters

Weak structure creates expensive gaps:

- payment disputes take too long to resolve
- weak records slow down follow-up
- accountability becomes harder to prove
- operators lose visibility as activity grows

## Who This Is For

## What A Better Workflow Looks Like

1. Driver record is created and verified clearly.
2. Vehicle assignment is structured and visible.
3. Payment or remittance state is easy to review.
4. Issues are handled with a real record, not an argument.
